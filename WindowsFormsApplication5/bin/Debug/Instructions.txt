Welcome to roll the ball game
to play this game you should make clear path from the start box to end box 
to do this you need to change the location of the scattered boxes to win the game
you would change locations between boxes by clicking one click on the first box
and another click in the other box. please make sure that the two boxes are beside 
each other,unless they will not move . you can open the harder levels by passing
levels  squentially.
